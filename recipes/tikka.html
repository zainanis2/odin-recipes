Tikka

Description

Tikka is a dish consisting of pieces of meat or vegetarian alternatives, with its origins tracing back to the ancient Babylon .The term 'tikka' was given in the mughal era .It is made by marinating the pieces in spices and yogurt, and cooking them in a tandoor.

Ingredients :

  • meat of choice
  • 1 teaspoon chilli powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon tumeric
  • 1/2 salt

Steps :

  1. mix the species and marinate meat
  2. skewer the meat
  3. grill it till tender
